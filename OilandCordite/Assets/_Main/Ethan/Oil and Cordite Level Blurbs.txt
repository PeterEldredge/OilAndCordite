Tutorial-
Even though you built the COM-Buster, it still takes a bit of practice to fly it well. Learn the basics of controlling and optimizing your flight in the COM-buster.


Village-
With your village under attack from The Collective, it is up to you to save them. Get in the COM-buster and destroy the bots before they can destroy your home.


City- 
Another mountain city is under attack. Race against the clock to save the city from falling into the smog sea.


Generators-
You are approaching The Collective’s base. Destroy the generators connected to their shield to unlock the entrance.


Final-
The final stretch of your run. Avoid the spinning spheres and the bots attacking you to make it into the center of the base and put a stop to The Collective’s activities.
